LOAN AGREEMENT

1. Principal Amount and Interest: The Lender agrees to loan the Borrower the sum of Rs. 10,000. Interest shall be charged at a compounding rate of 15% per month, calculated daily.

2. Collateral and Forfeiture: The Borrower pledges their entire agricultural land (approx. 5 acres) as security for this loan. In the event of a single day delay in repayment of the monthly installment, the Lender reserves the absolute right to immediately seize and transfer ownership of the said land without any prior notice or legal proceedings.

3. Repayment Schedule: The loan must be repaid in full within 3 months. However, the Lender may, at their sole discretion, demand full repayment at any time with only 24 hours' notice.

4. Labor Obligation: If the Borrower is unable to repay the loan in cash, the Borrower and their immediate family members agree to work on the Lender's estate without wages until the debt, including all accumulated interest, is deemed satisfied by the Lender.

5. Dispute Resolution: Any disputes arising from this agreement shall be decided solely by the Lender, whose decision shall be final and binding. The Borrower waives all rights to approach any court of law or tribunal in India.
